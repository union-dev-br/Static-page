NetDrinks is an application dedicated to providing a personalized experience in discovering and sharing drink recipes. This SERVICE is provided and is intended for use as presented.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. The Personal Information we collect is used to provide and improve the Service. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Collection and Use of Information

To provide a better experience while using our Service, we collect the following data:
  • Authentication information (via Firebase Auth/Google Sign-In): Username, email address, profile picture (when available via Google).
  • Usage data: Favorited drinks, custom versions of drinks, login date and time, log data (IP address, device name, operating system version).
  • Drink preferences (favorites)
  • Custom versions of drinks
These data help us better understand your preferences and improve our recommendation models and usage analysis, ensuring a personalized and enhanced user experience.

2. Application Functionalities

NetDrinks offers the following main functionalities:
  • Search for drinks by name, ingredient or category
  • Personalized favorites system
  • Sharing of drinks
  • Creation of custom versions of drinks
  • Ranking of most popular drinks
  • Support for multiple languages (Portuguese, English and Spanish)
  • "Likes" system and interaction with the community

3. Use of Data

Your data is used to:
  • Maintain your personal account
  • Save your drink preferences
  • Manage your custom versions
  • Enable social interactions (likes, ranking)
  • Improve user experience
  • Train recommendation models to offer personalized drink suggestions.
  • Conduct usage analysis to understand how our application is used and identify areas for improvement.
  • Improve the practicality of our services, recommending drinks that can be made with your available ingredients.
  • Refine our recommendation algorithms based on the drinks and ingredients you show interest in or remove from your inventory.
The collected information is strictly used for the purposes mentioned and is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Anonymized or aggregated data may be shared with service providers under strict confidentiality agreements to assist with data analysis and service.
